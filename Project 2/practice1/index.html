Lesson 1: Getting Started

In this lesson, you will learn the basics of car riding, including how to adjust your seat, mirrors, and steering wheel for comfort and control.

Practice Activity: Adjust the seat, mirrors, and steering wheel in your car according to the instructions provided in the lesson.

Lesson 2: Driving Techniques

This lesson covers essential driving techniques such as accelerating, braking, and turning. You'll also learn about maintaining a safe following distance and using turn signals.

Practice Activity: Practice driving in a parking lot or quiet street, focusing on smooth acceleration, braking, and turning. Remember to use your turn signals.

Lesson 3: Safety Tips

Safety is paramount when it comes to car riding. This lesson will teach you about defensive driving strategies, how to handle emergencies, and the importance of wearing seat belts.

Practice Activity: Review the safety tips provided in the lesson and create a checklist of safety measures to follow every time you get behind the wheel.
